## 1. GRANT OF PERMISSION

The Client grants Onix Media a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use the following types of content ("Media") provided by the Client:

- Photographs (action shots, headshots, training photos, team photos)
- Video footage (match highlights, training clips, interview recordings)

- Logos or badges from teams/clubs/schools (subject to those organizations' own rights)
- Any other media explicitly provided by the Client for website use

## 2. PERMITTED USES

Onix Media may use the Media for the following purposes:

a) Display on the Client's personal athlete website.
b) Display on the Onix Media website (www.onixmedia.agency) as a portfolio or example, unless the Client opts out in writing.
c) Promotion of Onix Media services on social media, email campaigns, and marketing materials.
d) Press or media coverage related to Onix Media's services.

## 3. RESTRICTIONS

Onix Media agrees NOT to:

a) Sell or license the Client's Media to third parties for profit.
b) Use the Media in a manner that is defamatory, misleading, or harmful to the Client's reputation.
c) Alter the Media in a way that misrepresents the Client.
d) Use the Media for purposes unrelated to the services described in the Website Service Agreement.

a) They own or have the legal right to provide all Media submitted to Onix Media.

c) If any Media was taken by a professional photographer or third party, the Client has obtained the necessary permissions.

Onix Media is not liable for any third-party claims arising from Media provided by the Client.

---

## 5. CREDITS & ATTRIBUTION

Where reasonably possible, Onix Media will credit photographers or content creators as requested by the Client.

The Client may request the removal of specific Media at any time by contacting contact@onixmedia.agency. Onix Media will remove the requested content within 14 business days.

Upon termination of the Website Service Agreement, all Media will be removed from the website and Onix Media's marketing materials within 30 days, unless otherwise agreed.
