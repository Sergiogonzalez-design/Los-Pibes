## 1. SERVICES

Onix Media agrees to design, build, and publish a personal athlete website on behalf of the Client. The website may include, but is not limited to:

- Personal biography and player profile
- Career statistics and season breakdowns
- Photo and video highlights
- Social media links
- Contact/recruiting inquiry form
- Career timeline

The specific content, design, and features will be agreed upon between both parties prior to launch.

---

## 2. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

The Client agrees to:

a) Provide accurate and truthful information, statistics, and biographical content.
b) Supply photos, videos, and any other media to be featured on the website.
c) Notify Onix Media of any changes to career information (transfers, new stats, etc.) in a timely manner.
d) Review and approve website content before it is published.

---

## 3. CONTENT OWNERSHIP

a) The Client retains full ownership of all personal content (photos, videos, statistics, biography) provided to Onix Media.
b) Onix Media retains ownership of the website design, code, and technical infrastructure.
c) Upon termination of this Agreement, Onix Media will provide the Client with all their personal content upon request within 30 days.

---

## 4. ONIX MEDIA RIGHTS

The Client grants Onix Media the non-exclusive right to:

a) Display the Client's website as an example of Onix Media's work on the Onix Media website (www.onixmedia.agency) and in marketing materials, unless the Client requests otherwise in writing.
b) Reference the Client's name and sport when promoting Onix Media services.
c) Make technical updates and improvements to the website as needed.

---

## 5. DURATION

This Agreement begins on the date of signing and continues until either party terminates it in writing with 30 days' notice.

---

## 6. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days' written notice to contact@onixmedia.agency. Upon termination:

a) The website will be taken offline within 30 days.
b) All Client content will be returned or deleted upon request.
c) Onix Media will remove the Client's website from any portfolio or example materials.
