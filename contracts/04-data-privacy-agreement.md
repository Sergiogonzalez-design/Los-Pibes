## 1. PURPOSE

Onix Media collects, stores, and processes personal information in order to provide athlete website creation services. This Agreement explains what data is collected, how it is used, and the Client's rights regarding their data.

---

## 2. DATA COLLECTED

Onix Media may collect and store the following personal information:

**From the Athlete directly:**
- Full name, date of birth, nationality
- Email address, phone number, postal address
- Sport, position, team/club/school affiliations
- Career statistics and performance data
- Photographs, videos, and other media
- Social media handles and links

**From the website (automatically):**
- Inquiries submitted through the contact form on the Athlete's website (name, email, sport, country, phone, age, message of third parties)
- Website analytics (page views, visit duration — no personally identifiable information)

---

## 3. HOW DATA IS USED

Personal data collected by Onix Media is used exclusively for:

a) Building and managing the Client's athlete website.
b) Communicating with the Client regarding their website and services.
c) Forwarding recruiting inquiries received through the website to the Client.
d) Improving Onix Media's services.
e) Portfolio and marketing purposes, as authorized in the Media & Image Release Agreement.

Onix Media will never sell personal data to third parties.

---

## 4. DATA STORAGE

Client data is stored securely using industry-standard services, currently including:

- **Supabase** (database hosting) — for contact form submissions and athlete data
- **Vercel** (website hosting) — for the athlete website itself

Both services maintain their own data security practices and privacy policies. Onix Media is not liable for breaches occurring within third-party infrastructure.

---

## 5. DATA RETENTION

Onix Media will retain the Client's data for the duration of the active service agreement. Upon termination:

a) Personal data will be deleted or returned to the Client within 30 days of a written request.
b) Contact form submissions from third parties (coaches, scouts) will be deleted or anonymized within 60 days of termination.

---

## 6. CLIENT RIGHTS

The Client has the right to:

a) **Access** — Request a copy of all personal data Onix Media holds about them.
b) **Correction** — Request corrections to any inaccurate data.
c) **Deletion** — Request complete deletion of their personal data ("right to be forgotten").
d) **Portability** — Request their data in a common format (e.g., CSV or PDF).
e) **Withdrawal of Consent** — Withdraw consent to data processing at any time, which will result in termination of services.

To exercise any of these rights, contact: contact@onixmedia.agency

---

## 7. THIRD-PARTY INQUIRIES

When a coach, scout, or third party submits an inquiry through the Client's website contact form, Onix Media will:

a) Store the inquiry securely in the Onix Media database.
b) Forward the inquiry to the Client's registered email address.
c) Not share the inquiry with any other third party.
d) Delete inquiries upon request or upon termination of service.

---

## 8. MINORS

For Athletes under 18 years of age, all data privacy rights may be exercised by their Parent or Legal Guardian, as documented in the Minor Athlete Parental Consent Addendum (Document 03).

Onix Media takes reasonable technical measures to protect personal data, including:

- Secure database access controls
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Restricted internal access to Client data
